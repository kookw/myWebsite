About Me

Working smart, being motivated and driven are what define me. I love experimenting with new ideas or concepts and have a curious mind.

Financial Technology Enthusiast

After learning about the wonders of technology after volunteering in the Singapore Fintech Festival in 2018, I discovered where my interests lie and changed course to Financial Informatics in Singapore Polytechnic. Since then, I have been harnessing skills and languages such as Python, R and Web Development. After years of study, I led my team to champion the PolyFinTech100 API Hackathon 2020 under Decentralised Finance! Hoping to apply my skills for the better good of society.
